Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about our webpage

Do I need the internet to play these games? +
No, our games are designed for local play, so you can enjoy them with your friends without needing an internet connection.
Can I play solo if my friends are not available?+
Yes! Most of our games also have a single-player mode so you can practice and have fun even when playing alone.
Are the games free to play?+
Yes, all the games on our platform are free to play and don’t require any hidden charges
Do I need to create an account to play? +
No, you can directly open the game and start playing without any account or signup.
Can I play these games on my mobile phone? +
Yes, our games are designed to work smoothly on both desktop and mobile devices.
Do the games save my progress or scores?+
Some games save your local scores and progress on your device, but since they are offline/local, scores are not stored online.
What kind of games are available here? +
You’ll find simple and fun games like Tic Tac Toe, puzzles, and board-style challenges that are perfect for quick matches with friends.
